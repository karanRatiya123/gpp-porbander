Admission Process

Follow these steps to apply for programs at GPPR CTE Guj.

Step 1: Online Application

Submit your application through our online portal with required documents.

Step 2: Document Verification

Upload and verify academic certificates, ID proofs, and other documents.

Step 3: Interview/Entrance Test

Attend an interview or entrance exam based on your program.

For detailed guidelines, refer to the official admission brochure.

Eligibility Criteria

Check if you meet the requirements for admission to GPPR CTE Guj programs.

Undergraduate Programs
  • Minimum 50% in 10+2 (Science/maths/social Science).
  • Valid entrance exam scores (e.g., CBSC,bord gujarat).
  • Age limit minimum: 16 years.
Postgraduate Programs
  • Bachelor's degree with 55% aggregate.
  • Relevant work experience preferred for some courses.
  • Entrance test or merit-based selection.

Contact admissions office for specific program requirements.

Entrance Exams & Interviews
